Committee


The Marathon of Parallel Programming exists because of the volunteering work of some enthusiastic person. The first edition was prepared by Wagner Meira Jr., from DCC/UFMG.

After that, a special committee was structured to help the local committee to prepare the Marathon, organizing and broadcasting the Call for Teams. After all, they build the problems set to be used in the contest.

The committee are (ordered by name):

  • Calebe de Paula Bianchini, Mackenzie, SP-BR
  • Raphael Camargo, UFABC, SP-BR
  • Emilio Francesquini, UFABC, SP-BR

It is very important to thanks every local committee that organized the Marathon over the past few years (see Old contests).
